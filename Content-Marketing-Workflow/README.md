# Content Marketing Workflow - Notion Template

A complete system for content creators to plan, create, and track their content marketing across all platforms.

## What's Included

### 1. Content Dashboard
- Total content pieces this month
- Platform breakdown
- Content status overview
- Upcoming deadlines
- Engagement trends

### 2. Content Calendar
- Monthly/weekly view
- Platform tags (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, Blog, Newsletter, Podcast)
- Content type (video, post, article, email, reel)
- Status (Idea, Researching, Writing/Recording, Editing, Scheduled, Published)
- Publishing date/time
- Assigned to (for teams)

### 3. Ideas Bank
- Raw content ideas
- Source (brainstorm, competitor, audience request, trending)
- Category/topic tags
- Status (Saved, In Progress, Scheduled, Published)
- Priority level
- Notes & research links

### 4. Research Library
- Competitor analysis
- Trending topics
- Audience pain points
- Keyword/topic clusters
- Reference links

### 5. Production Pipeline
- Each piece tracks through stages:
  - Idea → Research → Outline → Draft → Review → Edit → Schedule → Publish
- Time spent tracking
- Assets needed (images, clips, links)
- Dependencies

### 6. Platform-Specific Guidelines
- Best posting times
- Hashtag sets
- Character limits
- Formatting tips
- CTA variations

### 7. Analytics Tracker
- Platform connections
- KPIs per platform
- Weekly/Monthly review templates
- Best performing content

## How to Use
1. Duplicate to your Notion
2. Fill in your content ideas
3. Move through production stages
4. Schedule and publish
5. Track analytics weekly
