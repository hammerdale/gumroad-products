## Step-by-Step Upload Process

### Step 1: Prepare Your Product Files
- [ ] Download files from GitHub: https://github.com/hammerdale/gumroad-products
- [ ] Convert .md files to PDF (use free tools like MarkdownConverter or copy to Google Docs → Download as PDF)
- [ ] Rename files professionally: `Real-Estate-Agent-Email-Templates-v1.pdf`

### Step 2: Create Product on Gumroad
1. Go to gumroad.com → Click "New Product"
2. Upload your PDF file
3. Fill in details (see below)

### Step 3: Optimize Each Listing

**Product Name:**
- Real Estate Agent Email Templates (Not "Email Templates")
- ChatGPT Prompts for Real Estate Agents (Not just "AI Prompts")
- Small Business Notion Workspace (Not just "Notion Template")

**Description Template:**
```
WHAT IS THIS:
[One sentence describing the product]

WHO IT'S FOR:
[Target audience - be specific]

WHAT'S INSIDE:
- [Bullet 1]
- [Bullet 2]
- [Bullet 3]

WHY IT WORKS:
[2-3 sentences on the problem it solves]

BONUS: [Any extras included]
```

**Example - Real Estate Templates:**
```
WHAT IS:
5 professionally written emails for real estate agents to close more deals.

WHO IT'S FOR:
- New real estate agents needing scripts
- Experienced agents wanting to save time
- Teams wanting consistent client communication

WHAT'S INSIDE:
- New Listing Announcement template
- Open House Invitation
- Follow-Up After Showing
- Market Update Newsletter
- Past Client Re-engagement sequence

WHY IT WORKS:
Real estate agents are busy. These copy-paste emails get responses without writing from scratch. Each template is proven to convert.

BONUS: Includes customization tips and best send times.
```

### Step 4: Set Pricing
- [ ] Check "Allow fixed price"
- [ ] Set price: **$29** (under market average = faster first sales)
- [ ] Leave "Pay what you want" OFF
- [ ] Add "Add a tip" - leave ON (extra revenue)

### Step 5: Cover Image (Critical!)
- Use free tool: Canva.com
- Size: 1280x720 ( Gumroad recommended)
- Include:
  - Product title
  - "PDF" or "Template" badge
  - Simple icon/graphic
  - Price in corner
- Colors: Use contrasting, clean design

### Step 6: Tags (Important for Discovery)
Add 5-8 relevant tags:
- Email templates
- Real estate marketing
- ChatGPT prompts
- Notion template
- Business tools
- Productivity
- Marketing templates
- Industry-specific

### Step 7: URL & Permalink
- Product URL: gumroad.com/l/[custom-slug]
- Set slugs:
  - `/l/real-estate-email-templates`
  - `/l/chatgpt-realtor-prompts`
  - `/l/notion-business-workspace`
