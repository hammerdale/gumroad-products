# ChatGPT Prompts for Real Estate Agents

## 20 AI Prompts to Automate Your Business

---

### Lead Generation
1. **Write a cold prospecting message** for [property type] buyers in [city]
2. **Create a follow-up sequence** for leads who didn't respond to initial outreach
3. **Generate geo-farming content** for [neighborhood]

### Listing Descriptions
4. **Write a compelling listing description** for a [bedroom] bedroom, [bathroom] bathroom home with [features]
5. **Create a luxury listing description** for a property over $[price]
6. **Generate a neighborhood description** highlighting [schools/amenities/transit]

### Marketing Content
7. **Write social media posts** for a new listing at [address]
8. **Create an email newsletter** about [market update/open house/new listing]
9. **Generate ad copy** for a [for sale/for rent] property

### Client Communication
10. **Write a thank you note** to past clients after closing
11. **Create a buyer consultation agenda** for a first meeting
12. **Draft a negotiation talking points** document for [situation]

### Administrative
13. **Write a vendor referral email** to contractors you trust
14. **Create a sphere of influence letter** introducing yourself
15. **Generate a testimonial request** to send after a successful closing

### Educational Content
16. **Create a "First-Time Homebuyer Guide"** outline
17. **Write a "How to Prepare Your Home for Sale"** brochure
18. **Generate a market report** for [neighborhood]

### Scripts & Objection Handling
19. **Write responses to common objections** like "We're not ready to buy yet"
20. **Create a pricing consultation script** for sellers worried about listing price

---

## How to Use These Prompts

1. **Copy the prompt** you need
2. **Replace the bracketed info** with your specific details
3. **Paste into ChatGPT** (free version works fine)
4. **Edit and personalize** the output
5. **Use in your business**

Pro tip: Save your best outputs as templates for future use.

---

## Bonus: Prompt Engineering Tips

- Be specific about the outcome you want
- Include context (who is the audience?)
- Specify tone (professional, friendly, urgent)
- Ask for multiple options when you need choices
