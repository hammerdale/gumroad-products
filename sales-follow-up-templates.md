## What's Inside

### Template 1: The "Nice to Meet You" Follow-Up
Subject: Great meeting you [Name]

Hi [Name],

It was great meeting you [yesterday/at the event]. I really enjoyed learning more about [their company/your challenges].

As discussed, here's [the resource/article/case study] I mentioned - [brief description].

Would love to [schedule a call/continue the conversation] to see how we might be able to help [Company].

Best,
[Your Name]

## How to Use These

1. **Personalize** - Replace [bracketed items] with your prospect's specific details
2. **Keep it short** - These are designed to be 3-5 sentences max
3. **Send within 24 hours** - While the conversation is still fresh
4. **Track responses** - Note which templates get the best replies
